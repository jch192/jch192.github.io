About Me

A native northerner, I escaped the cold just before beginning my undergraduate studies and have managed to live in continually warmer places since. I graduated magna cum laude from the University of South Carolina with a degree in business and a minor in retailing in 2013. I wrote for and became an editor of the school's magazine, Garnet and Black, a position that truly sparked my love for journalism. From there, I moved down to the Big Easy (New Orleans, for those who aren't familiar), where I managed a three-story boutique fitness studio. I am passionate about health and wellness, and learned a wealth of information from some of the most knowledgable in the fitness industry through that experience. Today, I am working towards a master's degree in journalism from the University of Miami with specific areas of interest in broadcast and filmmaking.
